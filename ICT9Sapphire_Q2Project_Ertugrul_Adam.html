A Gallery of Football Stadiums to go to
The Philippines is home to many Basketball courts in Southeast Asia, but I believe Football deserves some recognition too! Here are some pictures of my most 3 favourite football stadiums here in our beautiful country, the Philippines!

The New Clark City Stadium

  • Is a multi-purpose stadium, and the Primary venue of New Clark City Sports Hub
  • Holds 20,000 seats, which makes this the most amount of seats in this list

The Philippine Sports Stadium

  • Is a football and track stadium located in Ciudad de Victoria
  • Capacity of 20,000-40,000
  • The biggest stadium in the country

The Rizal Memorial Stadium

  • The most popular stadium in the country
  • Holds up to 12,873 seats for people to watch the beautiful game
  • Is located in Rizal
